We

We give you the best for your animals.

Arthur Grain and Tuber farms manufacture animal feed that meets all nutritional requirements for all farm animals for maximum production.

Our business has established a strategic partnership with top notch suppliers like Frankatson, Louis Drefus, Safrip Ghana, Vestor Oil mills and Sonal Ghana.

These collaborations have led to establish and maintain a reputation of reliability and consistency of quality and service over the years.

Today, we are proud to serve our customers in the central and western region of Ghana combining quality that exceeds their expectations with competitive pricing and excellent customer service.
